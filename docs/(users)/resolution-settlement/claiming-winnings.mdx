import { Steps, Step } from 'fumadocs-ui/components/steps'

## Ways to claim

- <strong>Portfolio claim card</strong>: claim multiple redeemable markets at once.
- <strong>Market order panel</strong>: use <strong>Claim winnings</strong> when shown.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Open claim surface

    Go to <strong>/portfolio</strong> or the specific resolved market.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Submit claim

    Click claim and sign the request.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Balance update

    After confirmation, funds are credited to your available balance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Example

- Buy 100 shares at $0.35 (cost $35)
- Outcome wins and settles
- Redeem value is $100
- Gross result: +$65 before fees
