import { Steps, Step } from 'fumadocs-ui/components/steps'

## Buy flow

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Select outcome

    Open the market and choose the side you want to back.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Set order details

    Choose market or limit, input amount/shares, and check the estimated cost and fee.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Submit

    Confirm and sign. Filled amounts appear in your portfolio position.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sell flow

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Choose position side

    In the same market, switch to <strong>Sell</strong>.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Pick execution mode

    Use market for instant exit, or limit to target a specific price.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Confirm sell order

    Sign and monitor fill progress in <strong>Portfolio → Open Orders</strong>.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You can close before resolution or hold through settlement and claim if your outcome wins.
