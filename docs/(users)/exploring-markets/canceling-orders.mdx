---
title: Canceling Orders
description: Cancel open limit orders from your portfolio
---

import { Steps, Step } from 'fumadocs-ui/components/steps'

## Cancel a single order

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Open Portfolio

    Go to <strong>/portfolio</strong> and switch to the <strong>Open Orders</strong> tab.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Find your order

    Use search/sort if needed and locate the active limit order.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Cancel

    Click <strong>Cancel</strong> on the order row.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Cancel all open orders

If available for your account, use the <strong>Cancel all</strong> action in the Open Orders tab to remove all active orders for your wallet.

<Callout type="info">
  Cancellation only affects unfilled quantity. Already-filled portions remain in your position history.
</Callout>
