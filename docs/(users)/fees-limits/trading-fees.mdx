## Current trading fee

- Current configured rate: <strong><TradingFeeDisplay /></strong>
- Source: live affiliate settings configured by the platform admin
- Display: fee estimate appears in the order flow before you submit

<Callout type="info" title="Fee formula">
  Trading fee (USDC) = order notional × <TradingFeeDisplay />.
</Callout>

## Market vs limit orders

- <strong>Market orders</strong> execute immediately and apply the configured trading fee.
- <strong>Limit orders</strong> apply fee when fills happen.
- There is no guaranteed maker rebate in this platform flow unless explicitly configured.

## Affiliate split

When a valid referral is attached to the order:

- Affiliate receives: <strong><AffiliateShareDisplay /></strong> of trading fee
- Platform retains: <strong><PlatformShareDisplay /></strong> of trading fee

If no referral is attached, the platform retains the full fee.
