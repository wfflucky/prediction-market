## Open notifications settings

Use <strong>/settings/notifications</strong>.

## Current notification controls

- <strong>In-app: Order fills</strong>
- <strong>Email resolutions</strong>
- <strong>In-app resolutions</strong>
- <strong>Hide small fills (&lt;1 share)</strong>

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Go to settings

    Open <strong>Settings → Notifications</strong>.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Change toggles

    Enable or disable the channels available for your deployment.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Confirm update

    Preferences are saved immediately after toggle changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>
