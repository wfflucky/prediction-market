## Portfolio tabs

In <strong>/portfolio</strong>, you can switch between:

- <strong>Positions</strong>: open holdings and mark-to-market view
- <strong>Open Orders</strong>: active limit orders waiting to fill
- <strong>History</strong>: executed activity and export tools

## Claim-ready markets

When you have redeemable winning positions, a claim card appears in Portfolio.
You can submit a single claim that covers all available markets.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Open Portfolio

    Visit <strong>/portfolio</strong>.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Check claimable markets

    If available, click <strong>Claim</strong> on the winnings card.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Sign transaction

    Confirm the claim request and wait for balance update.
  </Step>
</Steps>
