## Market order execution mode

Open <strong>/settings/trading</strong> to configure your preferred execution behavior:

- <strong>Fill and Kill (FAK)</strong>: fill available size now, cancel unfilled remainder.
- <strong>Fill or Kill (FOK)</strong>: require full immediate fill, otherwise cancel.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Open trading settings

    Go to <strong>Settings → Trading</strong>.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Choose mode

    Select FAK or FOK based on your execution preference.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Save

    Changes apply to future market orders.
  </Step>
</Steps>
