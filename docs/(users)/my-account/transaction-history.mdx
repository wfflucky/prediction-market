## Where history lives

Open <strong>/portfolio</strong> and select the <strong>History</strong> tab.

You can search, filter, and sort activity such as trades and other wallet actions captured by the platform.

## Export CSV

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Filter your view

    Apply search/type/sort filters in the History tab.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Export

    Click the <strong>download</strong> icon to export a CSV of the currently visible rows.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Store securely

    Keep exported files safely for tax, accounting, or compliance workflows.
  </Step>
</Steps>
