## Enable 2FA

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Open 2FA settings

    Go to <strong>/settings/two-factor</strong>.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Scan setup QR

    Use an authenticator app (for example, Google Authenticator/Authy), then enter the 6-digit code.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Optional: trust this device

    You can mark the device as trusted for a limited period after activation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Disable 2FA

Return to the same page and use <strong>Disable 2FA</strong> if you need to remove it.

<Callout type="warning">
  If you lose access to your authenticator app, recovery may require additional verification.
</Callout>
