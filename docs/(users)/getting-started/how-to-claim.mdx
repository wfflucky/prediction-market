## Claim workflow

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Wait for resolution

    Open a resolved market and confirm your outcome is the winner.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Start claim

    Use <strong>Claim</strong> from the Portfolio claim card, or
    <strong>Claim winnings</strong> inside the market panel when available.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Approve and confirm

    Sign the claim transaction request and wait for confirmation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Prerequisite">
  Your trading setup (proxy wallet + required auth/approvals) must be completed before claiming.
</Callout>

After claim confirmation, funds are credited to your available balance and can be reused for new trades
or withdrawn.

<Callout type="success" title="Need deeper resolution details?">
  See <strong>Resolution &amp; Settlement</strong> for timelines, dispute windows, and edge cases.
</Callout>
