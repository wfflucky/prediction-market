## Open the deposit flow

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Open wallet actions

    Click <strong>Deposit</strong> in the header.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Pick a funding method

    Choose one of the available options: <strong>Wallet</strong>, <strong>Buy Crypto</strong>,
    or <strong>Transfer Funds</strong>.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Confirm and wait

    Approve the transaction (when required) and wait for confirmation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Funding methods

- <strong>Wallet</strong>: bridge/swap supported assets from your connected wallet.
- <strong>Buy Crypto</strong>: use card/bank methods through the integrated on-ramp provider.
- <strong>Transfer Funds</strong>: send USDC on Polygon to your displayed deposit address (copy or QR).

<Callout type="warning" title="Network and token check">
  Always send the exact token/network shown in the deposit modal. Sending unsupported assets
  or the wrong network can permanently lose funds.
</Callout>

## USDC naming note

Depending on provider/route, you may see token labels like <strong>USDC</strong> or <strong>USDC.e</strong>.
Follow the exact token shown in your current transfer flow.

## Test-mode note

If your deployment is in test mode, the deposit modal may show a faucet path to get test USDC.
