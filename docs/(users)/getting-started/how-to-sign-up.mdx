## Create your account

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Open the auth modal

    Click <strong>Sign Up</strong> or <strong>Log In</strong> in the header.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Connect and authorize

    Choose your wallet in the connection modal and approve the signature request.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Finish setup

    Once connected, your session is active and you can open markets, fund your account, and trade.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## First trade requirement

Before your first order, <SiteName /> may ask you to complete a one-time trading setup
(proxy wallet + required approvals). The UI guides you through each step.
